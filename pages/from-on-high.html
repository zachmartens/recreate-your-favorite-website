Welcome to BANR,

One of the largest and most influential corporations in the world, where the key to success lies in productivity and a commitment to hard work. Set in an eccentric 80s office, From on High invites audiences to join the absurd grind of BANR’s work environment and culture of devotion. Residing in an iconic 9,000 sq ft warehouse in the RiNo Arts District, audiences enter as new employees of BANR at the start of their work week. Throughout the 90 minute performance, they are free to explore the office, interact with their coworkers, and uncover the depths of devotion that BANR requires.

We quickly learn that to be successful means hard work, company over colleague, and following the guidance from BANR’s founder and CEO, He-Himself. Soon, rumors begin to swirl that later in the week, He-Himself plans to visit Level 3 and his beloved staff. Wrapped in speculation, each employee pushes a little harder and works a little longer, aiming to outshine their competition. This could be the chance to prove their worth to the company as all roads lead to week’s end.
